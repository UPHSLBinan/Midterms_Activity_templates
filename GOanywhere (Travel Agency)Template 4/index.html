Weekend in boracay   

Many tourists who wish to experience the island's elegance directly travel to Boracay because of its magnificent landscapes. The island is known for its pristine blue waters, fine white sand, a variety of flora and wildlife, and abundant marine life. No surprise Conde Nast Traveler and Travel+Leisure consistently rank it as one of the top islands in the world.

The Best Tours

These are the best tours that we can offer if you want to maximize your exeprience here in the country. Choose the best option that you think will suit you best.

Bicol Tour 

Bicol is a beautiful place where you can find all sorts of things to do. There are historical sites, eco-tourism spots, and beautiful waterfalls. Plus, there are lots of animals like whales and manta rays that you can see.

Siargao Tour 

Siargao has a distinct atmosphere, and its allure extends beyond its scenic surroundings. Siargao is stunning, and the Maasin River and the Magpupungko Pools are fantastic sights, but there are also a ton of other things to do on the island.

Laguna Tour

The Laguna province is blessed with a wealth of natural resources, including a large number of mineral hot and cold springs, magnificent waterfalls, and protected forest regions rich with flora and animals. The province is home to a significant collection of ecotourism, wellness, and leisure travel destinations.

Palawan Tour

With mountains rising from the ocean and crystal-clear turquoise waters surrounded by palm-lined white sandy beaches, its dramatic views are magnificent from sea level. There is nothing else quite like it, and the underwater world also offers an extraordinary experience, with world-class scuba diving and even WWII shipwrecks to explore.

Amazing boracay experience  

If you haven't visited the adjacent islands, you haven't truly experienced Boracay. Plan an island-hopping journey to explore Boracay's top beaches. Spend an entire day discovering the natural splendor of the adjacent islands and beaches, such as Carabao Island, Hinugtan Beach, Magic Island, Piknikan Island, and Crystal Cove Island, to name a few. Boracay Amazing Tour

In Boracay, adventure has no age restrictions. Feel the wind on your face, ride the waves in style, and try something new. The island provides a variety of adrenaline-pumping activities that will satisfy your longing for something thrilling. Scuba diving, snorkeling, kiteboarding, stand-up paddling, and beach activities. Additionally, you may have a blast with your family and friends by participating in exciting water sports like helmet diving and riding a banana boat or UFO. If you want to take things to the limit, you may try parasailing, which gives you a bird's-eye view of the entire island while being pushed by a boat. Your trip to Boracay will be unforgettable for sure if you participate in these activities!

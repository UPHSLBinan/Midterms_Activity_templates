Rooms & Appartment

Discover the comforts of home away from home with HOTtel's Apartment Suites. Enjoy spacious living areas, fully-equipped kitchens, private bedrooms, and a range of amenities designed for extended stays.
Food & Restaurant

Satisfy your cravings with the delectable food and beverage options at HOTtel. From our breakfast buffet to our in-room dining, we offer a variety of culinary delights to satisfy every palate.

Relax and rejuvenate at HOTtel's spa and wellness services. Our expert therapists offer a range of treatments and therapies to help you unwind and recharge. From massages and facials to yoga and fitness classes, we have everything you need to feel refreshed and revitalized during your stay.

At HOTtel, we offer a range of Sports & Gaming services to keep you entertained during your stay. At HOTtel, we offer exceptional Event & Party services to help you celebrate your special occasions in style.

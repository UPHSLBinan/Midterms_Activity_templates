About Us

Valorant VCT stands for Valorant Champions Tour, which is a competitive esports tournament for the popular first-person shooter game, Valorant. The VCT is organized by Riot Games, the creators of the game, and features teams from all over the world competing for a chance to become the Valorant world champions.

The tournament is divided into different stages, including Challengers, Masters, and the ultimate event, the Valorant Champions. During each stage, teams compete in a series of matches and tournaments to earn points and secure their spot in the next stage of the tournament. The top teams from each region qualify for the Valorant Champions event, where they compete for a share of a large prize pool and the title of Valorant world champions.

The VCT is an important event for both professional and aspiring players, as it provides an opportunity to showcase their skills and compete against the best players in the world. The tournament also attracts a large audience of esports fans, who watch the matches live or online and follow their favorite teams and players throughout the event.

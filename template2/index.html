How to build

Streetbike Concept, 03/03/2023  

If you want a streetbike concept buy lightened parts. like rotor disc, hub , frontshocks, swingarm, shifter. use slim tires and rims. use open pipe and modified engine. remove sidemirror,grab bar and stockparts. use orig parts much better if made in thailand because you are making a thaiconcept motorcycle. Use this orig parts like brembo caliper, brembo levers, branded tires like eatmydust, veerubber, and IRC maxxing tires. it is important for your safety. as much as possible use open pipe like DAENGsai4, CHArama9, NOOlamluka pipe. you can use also powerpipe like apido,organ sport, KOU. Use branded rims like D.I.D dirtstar, superdragrims (SDR), takasago excel asia(TEA). Use rare parts if you want to win in motorshows.

Motorshows 

Advice to win a motorshow, 04/04/2023

First, the obvious: have a well-executed unit with quality, rare and one-off parts along with a clean unit from exterior, interior, engine, and wheel/ rims cleaned. Regardless of the judging standard, always provide a well-structured spec sheet of the unit mods along with a listing of any accolades and awards. Canopies and lighting can also help with display points and add to the overall appearance to set one apart from the competition.

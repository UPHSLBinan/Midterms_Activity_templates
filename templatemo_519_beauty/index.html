ABOUT THE BRAND

Our new, updated line features all of my favorite iconic Lip Kit shades as well as my must-haves including blushes, bronzers, highlighters, high glosses, matte liquid lipsticks, lip blushes, brow essentials and eyeliners. Everything is clean, vegan, with ingredients that are good for your skin, but I made sure we didn’t sacrifice quality, pigmentation or performance. For me, being able to have a lasting matte lip formula that wasn’t overly drying, was so important when reformulating my Lip Kit. My new formula has 8 hour wear and lasting color but is super comfortable on the lips. I’m obsessed with it, and I know you guys are going to love it too. And this is just the start! I’m working on some amazing new launches that I can’t wait to share with you.

Welcome, Perpetualites!

Shengwei Education Consulting Services Corporation has been authorized by UPHSL as an exclusive partner to recruit students for International Transfer Pathway Program


For local and international students who are thinking of studying in the Philippines and in California United States of America, a great way to begin your journey is through an International Transfer Pathway Program.

The program allows students to earn a degree via a twinning program in two universities; the:

  • (1) University of Perpetual Help System Laguna (UPHSL), a premier university in the Philippines and
  • (2) Sonoma State University (SSU), one of the campuses of California State University located in Sonoma, California, United States of America

As part of UPHSL’s commitment to curricular diversity and to build opportunities for students to achieve pathways to a baccalaureate degree, University of Perpetual Help System Laguna has authorized Shengwei Education Consulting Services Corporation as its exclusive partner to recruit Chinese students for a guaranteed admission track.

UPHSL receives a plaque of recognition from CHED

The Commission on Higher Education (CHED) awarded the University of Perpetual Help System Laguna a plaque of recognition. This was given to formally acknowledge UPHSL’s efforts in providing free training on flexible learning for Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) during the implementation of CHED’s Hi-Ed Bayanihan Project.

Even prior to the pandemic, UPHSL has always championed online teaching as a sustainable strategy for modern education. It is even more crucial in the ‘next normal’, as proven by the disruptive events brought on by COVID-19. Teachers must learn how to navigate an online mode of instruction efficiently and confidently.

In response to this need, UPHSL developed a Comprehensive Training Program using Moodle as Learning Management System (LMS) with Theoretical Assessment, Practical Assessment, and Certification. Recognizing this initiative, the CHED Hi-Ed Bayanihan Project tapped UPHSL as a facilitator of a free webinar on the smooth transition to hybrid learning for the AY 2020-2021. This was attended by faculty members from HEIs all over CALABARZON.

Meet the Speakers

Son Goku

Considered as the Strongest Saiyan. He is known for his incredible strength and his ability to transform into different forms, each more powerful than the last. Goku is a beloved character among fans for his determination, his love of fighting, and his kind heart.

Naruto Uzumaki

The Seventh Hokage. Naruto is known for his energetic and optimistic personality, his determination to never give up, his positive influence to people around him, and his powerful ninja techniques, including the famous Rasengan.

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates with the goal of finding the One Piece and become the Pirate King. He gained the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit, and he uses this power to fight powerful enemies and protect his crew.

Ichigo Kurosaki

He gains the power of a Soul Reaper, which allows him to fight evil spirits and protect his friends and family. Ichigo is known for his strong sense of justice, his determination to protect those he cares about, and his unique sword-fighting style.

Edward Elric

The Fullmetal Alchemist. He and his brother Alphonse attempted to perform human transmutation, but the experiment goes horribly wrong and Edward loses his arm and leg. He becomes a State Alchemist to find a way to restore his and his brother's bodies.

Izuku Midoriya

In a world where most people have superpowers known as Quirks, Izuku is born without one, but dreams of becoming a hero like his idol All Might. After proving himself to All Might, he is granted One For All, the most powerful quirk.

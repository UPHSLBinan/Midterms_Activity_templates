Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games.

It was released for Microsoft Windows in June 2020 and for other platforms like PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch in the future.

Gameplay

Valorant features a five-on-five gameplay where each player selects a "agent" character before the match starts, each with unique abilities that can be purchased before the match starts or earned throughout it.

The game is a round-based shooter, with each round lasting one minute and thirty seconds. The attacking team is tasked with planting a bomb at one of two sites while the defending team tries to stop them. If the attacking team successfully plants the bomb and it explodes, they win the round. If the defending team successfully defuses the bomb, they win.

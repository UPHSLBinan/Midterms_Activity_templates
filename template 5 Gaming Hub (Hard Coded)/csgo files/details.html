CS:GO

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a multiplayer first-person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation.

It was released for Microsoft Windows, macOS, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3 in August 2012, and later for Linux in September 2014.

Gameplay

CS:GO features two teams, the Terrorists and the Counter-Terrorists, who compete against each other to complete objectives or eliminate the other team.

The game has several game modes including Bomb Defusal, Hostage Rescue, and Arms Race. In Bomb Defusal, the Terrorists must plant a bomb at one of two bomb sites while the Counter-Terrorists try to prevent them. If the bomb is successfully planted and explodes, the Terrorists win. If the Counter-Terrorists successfully defuse the bomb or eliminate all the Terrorists, they win the round.

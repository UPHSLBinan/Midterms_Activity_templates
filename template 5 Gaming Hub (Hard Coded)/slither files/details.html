Slither.io

Slither.io is a popular multiplayer online game where players control a snake-like avatar and move around a virtual space, eating pellets and other players to grow bigger and longer.

The game was released in 2016 and is available to play for free on web browsers and mobile devices.

Gameplay

Players start off as small snakes and must collect pellets scattered throughout the virtual space to grow in size. They can also eat other players' snakes by causing them to crash into their own snake's body, causing the other player to die and leaving behind pellets for the player to collect.

The game has no time limit or endpoint, and the objective is simply to grow the longest snake possible.
